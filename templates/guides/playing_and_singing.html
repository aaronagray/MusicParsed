Playing and singing at the same time

Playing and singing at the same time requires a lot of practice and coordination.

Make sure you’re playing and singing in the same key.

  • This requires finding the starting note for singing the song. Eventually, you’ll be able to do so by ear - after playing a few chords in the song, you’ll just “know” what to sing.
  • However, if you can’t do this yet, try matching a recording.

    Here are some tips:

    • Practice just playing. Get comfortable transitioning between chords with your left hand and strumming with your right hand.
    • Play along with a Youtube video. You can slow it down to 0.5 speed.
    • Cynthia Lin has lots of ukulele tutorials.
  • I post some covers and song chords.
    • Sing only a couple of words of the song while playing.
