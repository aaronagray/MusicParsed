Beginner Ukulele Songs

Once you learn the beginner ukulele chords (Am, C, F, G), you can play a ton of four chord songs. I made giant list of four chord songs ordered by chord progression.

Here are some other song recommendations that don't use many chords.

  • Riptide - Most of the song is Am, G, C.
  • Leaving on a Jet Plane - Only uses three chords G, C, D. I like Cynthia Lin's tutorial.
  • You Belong with Me - Only uses G, D, Am, C.
  • Yellow Submarine - Only uses Am, D, G.
